Frequently Asked Questions

Can my accountant help me in APARBooks?
Yes, our system supports multiple users logging in and operating simultaneously. You can also set different permissions for other accounts. In the Growing Plan and Premium Plan, we offer several preset roles, including Administrator, Manager, Accountant, and Bookkeeper.
What's the difference between construction accounting and construction management software?
Construction accounting software focuses specifically on the financial aspects of construction projects. It helps manage budgets, track expenses, handle invoicing, and ensure compliance with financial regulations. construction management software handles project planning, scheduling, and team collaboration. While accounting software deals with finances, management software oversees the entire project process.
How do I choose the right plan for my business?
We offer three plans to cater to different business sizes: If your company is a micro business with around 1-3 employees, we recommend the Basic Plan.
If your company is small to medium-sized with a dedicated person handling records, we suggest the Growing Plan.
If your company is medium-sized with more employees involved in accounting and needing more granular permissions, the Premium Plan is ideal for you.
Does APARBooks work on mobile?
Yes, APARBooks is web-based and can be accessed from any internet-enabled platform or device. As long as your device can access our website via a browser, you can use APARBooks seamlessly. We recommend using Google Chrome for the best experience.
Does APARBooks offer support to its customers?
Yes, we offer professional live chat support from Monday to Friday, 8 AM to 5 PM Pacific Time. Outside of these hours, you can leave a message, and our customer service team will respond as soon as possible during working hours.
Is APARBooks suitable for my project?
Absolutely! APARBooks is designed for small to mid-sized construction projects, typically with contract prices between $100K and $5M. We've crafted our software to fit the specific needs of these projects, making sure you get the best value and efficiency.
